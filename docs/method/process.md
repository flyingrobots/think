# Process

Think follows [METHOD](https://github.com/flyingrobots/method). See that repo's README for the full cycle loop, backlog operations, and playback discipline.

## Local conventions

- Cycles are numbered sequentially starting at `0001`.
- Historical milestones (M0–M5) and their retrospectives predate METHOD and remain in `docs/retrospectives/` and `docs/design/` as the archival record. New work uses METHOD structure.
- Design docs for active cycles live in `docs/design/<cycle>/`.
- Retrospectives for completed cycles live in `docs/method/retro/<cycle>/`.

## Guardrails

These are standing constraints inherited from the original design doctrine. They are not backlog items — they are boundaries.

- No embeddings or clustering before capture habit is proven.
- No dashboard-first UX.
- No tagging, ontology, or classification that pressures the user during capture.
- No autonomous reflection narration.
- No proactive AI interruptions during capture.
- No hosted collaborative thinking (deferred indefinitely).
- No public sharing surface (deferred indefinitely).
