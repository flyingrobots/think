# Replay session artifacts and reflection packs

## Replay session artifacts

Allow a future reflective session to generate a compact replay artifact showing the seed entries considered, the questions asked, the user's responses, and the resulting meta-entry or conclusion. Useful for "how did I get here?" moments.

## Reflection packs

Generate a "reflection pack" around a cluster or theme: a few raw entries, a few unresolved tensions, one or two sharp questions. Good candidate for later dialogue mode once enough real data exists.
